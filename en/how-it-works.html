Detailed Process

Detailed information about the creation process of your personalized coloring book

1

Placing an Order

To start the process, select the coloring book model you want from our website. Determine which of our 10, 12, 14, or 16 image models is most suitable for you.

By clicking the "Buy Now" button on the product page, you will be directed to the Shopier secure payment system. Here, you can complete your payment by entering your personal information and delivery address.

After your payment is completed, your order confirmation will be sent to your email address.

Tips:

  • Make sure you enter your delivery address correctly.
  • You can specify your special requests in the order notes.
  • If you experience any problems during the payment process, you can reach us through our contact page.
2

Photo Submission

After your order is confirmed, our team will contact you via email or WhatsApp. At this stage, you will be asked to send the photos you want to be included in your coloring book.

Depending on the model you choose (10, 12, 14, or 16 images), you need to send that number of photos. Sending your photos in high resolution will increase the quality of the drawings.

Tips:

  • Choose clear, high-resolution, and well-lit photos.
  • Prefer close-up photos where faces are clearly visible.
  • Avoid very dark, blurry, or very small photos.
  • You can specify the order in the book by numbering your photos.
3

Drawing and Printing

After receiving your photos, our professional illustrators begin to carefully transform each photo into lines. This process is entirely handmade, and each drawing is unique.

After the drawings are completed, our quality control team examines each drawing in detail, checking the quality of the drawings and their similarity to the photos.

Approved drawings are printed on high-quality 110-140gsm paper and made into a book. Your book is prepared durably with paperback binding.

Information:

  • The drawing process may take 2-3 days, depending on the number of photos.
  • Each drawing is prepared with lines suitable for a coloring book while remaining faithful to your photo.
  • The printing process is done on special coloring papers and is ideal for coloring.
4

Delivery

After your book is prepared, it is carefully packaged and sent to your address with our contracted shipping company. The tracking number is shared with you via email or WhatsApp.

Delivery time may vary depending on your location, but generally, your book will be in your hands within 1-3 days.

After receiving your book, you can start coloring right away! You can color with colored pencils, markers, or watercolors.

Tips:

  • You can track your shipment in real-time with the tracking number.
  • If you experience any problems during delivery, you can notify us immediately.
  • We recommend colored pencils for coloring, but markers and watercolors can also be used.

Photo Selection Tips

What you need to consider when selecting photos for the best results

High Resolution

Choose the highest resolution photos possible. Low-resolution or pixelated photos may reduce the drawing quality.

Good Lighting

Choose well-lit photos. Very dark or very bright photos may cause details to be lost.

Clear Faces

Choose photos where faces are clearly visible. Blurry or photos taken from too far away may not be suitable for drawing.

Close-Up

Prefer close-up photos if possible. Details may be lost in photos taken from too far away.

Expressive Faces

Photos with smiling or expressive faces create more lively and fun drawings.

Variety

Add variety to your coloring book by choosing photos with different poses, locations, and activities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to questions you may have about the process

How long will it take for my book to be ready after I place an order?

After receiving your photos, your coloring book will be prepared and shipped within 3-5 business days. The total process, including sending photos and shipping time, may take approximately 5-8 days.

How should I send my photos?

After you place your order, we will contact you via email or WhatsApp. You can send your photos in high resolution through these channels.

How similar will the drawings be to the photos?

Our illustrators make drawings as faithful as possible to your photos. However, some details may be simplified to fit the coloring book format. Distinctive features such as facial features, hair style, and clothes are preserved.

What colors can I use in my book?

Our books are suitable for coloring with colored pencils, markers, and watercolors. The paper quality is thick enough to preserve color marks. We recommend colored pencils for the best results.

Can I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order before the drawing process begins. Cancellation cannot be made after the drawing process has started. Please contact us as soon as possible for your cancellation requests.

Can I specify my special requests?

Yes, you can specify your special requests when sending your photos. For example, you can communicate requests such as emphasizing certain details or adding/removing certain elements.
