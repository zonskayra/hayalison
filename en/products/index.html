Frequently Asked Questions

How do I send my photos?

After your purchase, we will contact you via email or WhatsApp to collect your photos. You can send them through WhatsApp, email, or a file sharing service.

What kind of photos should I send?

Clear photos with good lighting work best. Close-up photos of faces and photos with simple backgrounds are ideal for creating detailed coloring pages.

How long does it take to receive my order?

After receiving your photos, we prepare your coloring book within 3-5 business days. Shipping typically takes 1-3 additional days depending on your location.

Can I see a preview before printing?

Yes, we can send you a digital preview of one or two pages for your approval before printing the entire book.
