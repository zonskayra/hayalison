Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about contacting us

How quickly do you respond?

We typically respond to messages sent through the contact form or email within 24 hours. During busy periods, this may extend to 48 hours.

How can I get information about my order?

To get information about your order, you can send an email with your order number or contact us via WhatsApp. You can also use the contact form.

I want to place a bulk order, how can I contact you?

For bulk orders, you can reach us by selecting "Collaboration" as the subject in the contact form. Alternatively, you can also contact us directly via email or phone.

How can I send my photos?

After your order is confirmed, you can send your photos via email or WhatsApp. For large-sized photos, you can also use file sharing services like WeTransfer.
