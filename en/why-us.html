Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to questions you may have

How should I send photos?

After you place your order, we will contact you via email or WhatsApp. You can send your photos in high resolution through these channels.

Which photos are suitable for drawing?

Clear, high-resolution, and well-lit photos are most suitable for drawing. Close-up photos where faces are clearly visible should be preferred. Very dark, blurry, or very small photos may not be suitable for drawing.

What is the delivery time?

After receiving your photos, your coloring book will be prepared and shipped within 3-5 business days. Shipping time may vary between 1-3 days depending on your location.

Which age groups is it suitable for?

Our coloring books are suitable for all age groups. While they are ideal for children aged 3-12, they can also be a relaxing activity for adults.

What are the payment options?

You can pay with credit card, debit card, and bank transfer. All payments are processed securely through Shopier.

What is your return and exchange policy?

Since these are personalized products, returns and exchanges are not accepted. However, if there is any manufacturing defect in the product, it will be replaced with a new one if you notify us within 7 days.
